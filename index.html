Main Information

The Flash is a superhero television series developed by Greg Berlanti, Andrew Kreisberg, and Geoff Johns, airing on The CW. It is based on the DC Comics character Barry Allen / Flash, a costumed superhero crime-fighter with the power to move at superhuman speeds.

The show follows Barry Allen, a forensic scientist with the Central City Police Department, who gains super-speed powers after a particle accelerator explosion. He uses his abilities to fight crime and protect his city as the superhero known as The Flash.
